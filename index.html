Spots

Spots was created to help people find study spots near them. I'm constatly googling where to study or "best study spots near me". I thought this app could be useful, especially for developers who dont have a WeWork membership. I had a lot of fun building this app and learned a lot of cool things along the way. Spots built with the Yelp Api, MapBox, & Firebase feel free to checkout a demo on youtube

Expensify

Expensify was built to help users manage their expenses. Expensify has full CRUD actions built with Firebase. Users can also sort by date or search by expense name. I decided to go with Google Authentication to allow users to get up and running right away because no one likes filling out forms. Check out how it works Expensify

Omnifood
